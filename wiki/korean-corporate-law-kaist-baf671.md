# Korean Corporate Law — KAIST BAF671 창업과 법

# Korean Corporate Law — 창업과 법 (BAF671)

Professor 류혁선, KAIST Graduate School of Finance. Open-book exam format.

## Business Judgment Rule (경영판단원칙)

### Korean Doctrine (3-Step Standard)
1. Director acted in good faith without material personal interest
2. Director was sufficiently informed
3. Decision was within reasonable business discretion

**Key articles**: 상법 제382조②, 제382조의3, 제399조①, 민법 제681조

### US Framework Comparison
| Concept | US (Delaware) | Korea |
|---|---|---|
| Default standard | BJR | BJR (judge-made, not statutory) |
| Conflicted transactions | Entire Fairness | 충실의무 + 자기거래 금지 |
| Reinstatement of BJR | MFW dual protection | No equivalent mechanism |

**Key US cases**: Smith v. Van Gorkom (process failure → liability), In re Walt Disney (business decision respected), MFW (dual protection restores BJR), Aronson v. Lewis

**Korean precedents**: 대법원 2001다52407, 2015다70044, 2003다69638, 2008다14633

### Criminal Intersection
Korea's 업무상 배임죄 creates criminal liability for business decisions (LBO contexts especially). Unpredictable and broader than civil BJR limits.

## Entire Fairness Doctrine
- Triggered when directors/controlling shareholders have material personal interest
- Two components: **Fair Dealing** (process) + **Fair Price** (substance)
- Origin: Weinberger v. UOP (1983)
- MFW exception: reverts to BJR if (1) independent special committee + (2) majority-of-minority vote

---

## Director Liability (이사의 책임)
Key duties: 선관주의의무 (duty of care) + 충실의무 (duty of loyalty)

### Liability to Company (상법 제399조)
Monitoring obligation — internal control system duties apply even to outside directors (대법원 2021다279347)

### Specific Prohibitions
- **경업금지** (Article 397): cannot compete without board approval; company has 개입권
- **기회유용금지** (Article 397-2, added 2011): no usurpation of corporate opportunities — triggered by Hyundai Glovis case
- **자기거래금지** (Article 398): self-dealing requires board approval

## Treasury Stock (자기주식) — 2026 Amendment
- **Key 2026 change**: Mandatory cancellation obligation (의무소각) — exam priority topic
- Exception for retention: requires explicit articles of incorporation provision (Article 341조의4, Clause 2, Item 5)
- Articles amendment: special resolution (1/3 attendance quorum + 2/3 approval)

---

## Corporate Veil Piercing (법인격 부인론)
Two doctrines:
1. **형해화** (sham formation) — company has no real independent existence
2. **남용** (abuse) — company used as tool to evade legal obligations

Applies to parent-subsidiary and newly established companies (판례).

---

## Capital Structure
- Three elements of a joint-stock company: 자본금, 주식, 주주유한책임
- 3% rule: listed company auditor election — each shareholder capped at 3% voting weight
- Small company exception: paid-in capital under 1 billion KRW

## Exam Strategy
- Always cite article numbers AND case numbers with ruling dates
- Professor flags 2026 amended provisions as high-priority traps
- True/false traps: single changed threshold numbers
- Study format: bold answer + one explanatory sentence with statute/precedent inline
